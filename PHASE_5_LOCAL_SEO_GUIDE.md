### NAP Consistency

**Name, Address, Phone must be IDENTICAL everywhere:**

```
Correct Format:
Name: Westminster Chariots
Address: Triangle, VA 22172
Phone: (571) 426-6338
Website: https://westminsterchariots.com
```

**Common Mistakes to Avoid:**
- ❌ Westminster Chariot (missing 's')
- ❌ WC Chariots (abbreviation)
- ❌ Westminster Chariots LLC (adding entity)
- ❌ 571-426-6338 (different phone format)
- ❌ http://westminsterchariots.com (http vs https)

**Consistency Checklist:**
- ✅ Exact business name
- ✅ Same address format
- ✅ Same phone format
- ✅ HTTPS website URL
- ✅ No extra spaces or punctuation

## 📋 Part 4: Review Management System

### Review Generation Strategy

**1. Automated Review Requests**
```
Timing: 24 hours after completed ride
Channel: Email + SMS
Template:
"Hi [Name], thank you for choosing Westminster Chariots! 
We'd love to hear about your experience. Please take a 
moment to leave us a review: [Google Review Link]"
```

**2. In-Person Review Request**
```
Chauffeur script:
"Thank you for riding with Westminster Chariots today. If you 
enjoyed your experience, we'd appreciate a Google review. 
Here's a card with more information."

Card includes:
- QR code to Google review
- Short URL
- Thank you message
```

**3. Email Follow-Up Sequence**
```
Day 1: Thank you email + review request
Day 7: Gentle reminder (if no review)
Day 14: Final reminder with incentive mention
```

**4. Incentive Program**
```
Offer: 5% off next ride for leaving honest review
Compliance: Clearly state "honest review" (not positive only)
Terms: One per customer, valid for 30 days
```
